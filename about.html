About

Meet Randa.

Randa Tighe is a Registered Practical Nurse and the only injector at The InjectR. Every client is assessed and treated by her, from the first consultation to the final result.

She works under physician medical direction, which means a physician provides the medical authorization behind every treatment on the menu. That oversight, her nursing registration, and full professional liability coverage are the reason to choose a nurse in a field with a lot of unqualified providers.

Her approach is conservative. She will tell you when a treatment is not right for you, and when doing less will look better. The work she is proudest of is the kind nobody can identify.

"Same girl, same injector. My before photo is only five years old. What changed was my mindset."
Randa Tighe, RPN
