FAQ

Is Randa a nurse? +

Yes. Randa Tighe is a Registered Practical Nurse registered with the College of Nurses of Ontario, working under physician medical direction.

Will I look overdone? +

The approach is conservative. Randa builds results gradually and recommends less when less is right.

Is there downtime? +

Most injectable treatments are quick with minimal downtime. Randa explains what to expect for each one at your consultation.

How much does it cost? +

It depends on the treatment and what your face actually needs, which is what the free consultation is for. You get clear pricing before anything is booked.

Where are you located? +
